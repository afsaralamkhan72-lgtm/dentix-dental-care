Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you treat children as well as adults?

Yes. We offer gentle pediatric dental care alongside our general dentistry services, so the whole family can be seen at one clinic.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring any previous dental records or X-rays if available, along with a list of current medications. If it's your first visit, just arrive a few minutes early to complete a short intake form.

Is the clinic registered and are the dentists licensed?

Yes. Our dentists are PMDC-registered, with registration numbers listed on their individual profiles on the About page.

What languages does your team speak?

Our team is comfortable in English, Urdu, and Pashto, so you can discuss your care in whichever language feels most natural.

Do you offer emergency dental care?

For urgent dental pain or trauma, please call or WhatsApp us directly at 0319-4580958 so we can advise on the fastest way to be seen.

How do I book an appointment?

Call us, message us on WhatsApp, or fill out the contact form on this site — whichever is easiest for you.

What sterilization/safety protocols do you follow?

We follow standard infection-control protocols, including instrument sterilization and single-use disposables where applicable, to keep every visit safe.

Do you offer root canal treatment in a single visit?

It depends on the case. Many root canals can be completed in one visit, but more complex cases may need two — your dentist will explain what's right for your tooth.

What's the difference between a filling and a crown?

A filling repairs a small area of decay or damage. A crown covers the entire visible tooth and is used when more of the tooth structure needs support or protection.

Do you treat dental anxiety or nervous patients?

Absolutely. Let us know when you book, and we'll take extra time to explain each step and keep the pace comfortable for you.

How often should I get scaling and polishing done?

Most patients benefit from professional cleaning every six months, though your dentist may suggest a different schedule based on your oral health.

Is parking available near the clinic?

Yes, street parking is available near Prime Plaza on Old Bara Road.
